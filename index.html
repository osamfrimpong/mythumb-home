What Makes myThumb Different? Benefits

Transparency

With myThumb voting platform the whole voting process is auditable end to end. The design of the system allows administrators to guarantee users that their votes are correctly issued and accounted for according to the intention to vote. In addition, a voting receiptis issued for each user.

Increase participation

myThumb e-voting system maximize user participation, by allowing them to vote from anywhere and allowing access from diferent computer systems and from any device that has an internet connection..

Save Cost

The use of a single system for the entire voting process significantly reduces the organizational and implementation costs.

Accessibility

myThumb e-voting platform provides a safe and private channel that allows all users to participate on equal terms. Increased accessibility for voters abroad and for persons with difculties in travelling or reduced mobility.

Precession

myThumb e-voting platform eliminates errors in manual count, transmission and tabulation which brings with it an accurate and quick publication of results, with receipt of vote for each vote cast.

Efficiency

myThumb e-voting platform can be used to conduct multiple elections concurrently. No need to reset systems to do next election.

How myThumb works?

Step 1

Create Your Elections.

An electoral administrator can create unlimited elections on the dashboard. It can be a single elections or even grouped elections with a single login for your voters. Elections can all run independently of each other at a time and date set.

Learn More

Step 2

Add Candidates & Voters.

Add candidates or bulk import candidate data, and assign each candidate to a position, Include Candidate photo, manifesto data or even video.

You control who is eligible to vote in your elections. Add voters one-by-one, or import them from a spreadsheet. myThumb asssigns each voter a unique voter ID.

Step 3

Launch Your Election

You are ready to go, launch your election and share the unique election URL with your voters. myThumb can email the url to eligible voters. For onsite voting, eligible voters can use their IDs to vote at a booth.

Learn More

Step 4

Real-time results & Statistics.

The Election results are generated instantly at the close of election, you may choose to publicly display your election results, or download them as a document for publishing.

Monitor how well your election is performing with interactive statistics on every position in the active online Election.
